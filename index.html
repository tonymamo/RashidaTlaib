About Rashida Tlaib

As a resident of Detroit and a product of Detroit Public Schools, I am committed to working to create a safer, cleaner, stronger community for all of us. Recently, I've been serving as a State Representative for the past five years. I am the only Michigan legislator with a fully-staffed service center—delivering more than ten unique, local programs to help working families build economic stability:

  • Helping over 400 families save their homes from tax foreclosure
  • Increasing access to weatherization programs.
  • Stopping utility shutoffs for hundreds of families.
  • Gaining better city services for thousands of residents.
  • Providing free tax preparation services to over 1,000 families, bringing in over $1 million in refunds.
  • Promoting childhood literacy by distributing over 9,000 free books.
  • Connecting hundreds of our youth with college admissions representatives and assisting graduating seniors with their financial aid applications.

I am the eldest of 14 and a child of immigrants, and I know the importance of hard work, honesty, and commitment to community. Both at the Capitol and in our neighborhoods, my priority is serving you and your family.

Whether it's strengthening consumer protections, helping to create block clubs, meeting with local officials to combat blight, fighting for environmental justice or taking other actions to make life better in our community, I will not stop working for you.

Sincerely,
